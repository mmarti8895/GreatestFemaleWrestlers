PWI Rankings

PWI content in this repository is intentionally partial and attribution-heavy (for example, top 10 entries per year). This keeps the project useful while avoiding full list reproduction.

All-time Curated Rankings

All-time rankings are curated and separated by promotion, using a transparent rubric.

  • Title impact (0-40): championship history and level of title achievements
  • Longevity (0-25): sustained in-ring relevance and tenure
  • Influence (0-20): historical impact, innovation, and legacy
  • Match quality (0-15): standout match output and critical reception

Total score = sum of all four categories (max 100).
